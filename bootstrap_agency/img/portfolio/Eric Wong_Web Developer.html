Journey

This is my story.

  • My dive into the world

    After graduating from Boston University with a degree in architectural studies, I set off to see the world. My first stop is teaching English in the lovely country of Japan.

  • Japan

    2014-2015

    Living in Yamagata Japan, I had the opportunity to travel around Asia and the Pacific, climb lots of mountains, and teach over 300 students.

  • Dark Days

    2015-2016

    This is when I entered graduate school. The days were long and the nights were very cold. Needless to say, I spent the majority of my time living in the library but managed to scrap away a shiny new degree in architectural history. It was here I also began to code.

  • Startup 2017

    Things are looking up!

    After a brief time in Taiwan to catch my breath, I dived deep into coding by joining Startup Institute. Here I developed my existing skills as well as honed-in on my passion to help and bring value to others through website design and data management.
